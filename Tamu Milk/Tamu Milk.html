Welcome to TAMU MILK DAIRY FARM

Tamu Milk Dairy Farm (T.M.D.F) was founded by Aisha Don in 2019.It started as a small scale company serving only the local people.It grew over the months by partnerships with other companies to the freater heights it is now. It is the best dairy farm in Kenya.Tamu Milk Dairy Farm is a business with a philosophies that emphasizes ethics, health, sustainability and viability. We’ve established an environmental friendly dairy farm that produces and distributes the best dairy milk products. Our products are delivered locally and to the neighbouring countries. We deal with milk products such as ice creams, butter, cheese, yogurt, ghee, condensed milk, dried milk and whey.Our products are processed using new technology. Our products are fully organic as there are no chemicals added to them ensuring health is on check for our consumers.Our customers joy is our satisfaction.We serve them with love.
